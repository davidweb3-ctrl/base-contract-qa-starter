What It Covers

Owner controls Authorized and unauthorized paths for privileged vault actions.
Pause safety Deposit behavior when the demo vault is paused or unpaused.
Signed claims Signer, recipient, amount, nonce, deadline, chain id, and contract binding.
Replay protection Nonce changes after a successful claim and reused signatures fail.
Deadline checks Expired signed actions fail with explicit test coverage.
Clear boundaries Public proof only. Not an audit framework and not a production package.
